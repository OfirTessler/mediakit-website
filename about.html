About Us

The story behind our mission to empower businesses and drive meaningful growth.

Our Story

Founded in 2000, Livluv Consulting started with a simple belief: every business deserves access to world-class strategic guidance. What began as a small advisory practice has grown into a trusted consulting firm serving over 150 organizations across industries.

Over two decades, we've helped companies navigate market disruptions, scale operations, embrace digital transformation, and build financial resilience. Our success is measured not by hours billed, but by the lasting impact we create for our clients.

Our Mission

To empower businesses of all sizes with actionable strategies, operational clarity, and the confidence to lead in an ever-changing market.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted consulting partner for growth-minded organizations, known for delivering measurable results and building lasting relationships.
